We are a small pop-up cafe business. About once per season we set up a little cafe in an interesting location for a week. We focus on creating pleasant atmospheres in unique places. To help foster creative conversation, all of our tables are covered in paper and we make custom scented crayons for people to draw with as they talk and drink their coffee.

A passionate and adventurous soul named Alex, embarked on a whimsical journey to bring a touch of magic to the world of coffee and culinary delights. The idea struck him like lightning one misty evening as he wandered through an old bookstore filled with dusty volumes and ancient tales. It was there that he stumbled upon a forgotten legend about a mischievous Little Ghost that would appear and disappear, leaving behind traces of wonder and mystery.

Captivated by this enchanting story, he decided to channel its spirit into a pop-up cafe concept, and thus, "Little Ghost" was born. The concept was simple yet magical—he wanted to create a cafe that would materialize in unexpected locations, bringing with it a sense of excitement and wonder. The menu, inspired by the ethereal qualities of the Little Ghost, would feature otherworldly treats and spectral delights.
